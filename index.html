Why get a hobby? It is scientifially proven that hobbies can reduce stress and improve mental health. 73% of people suffering from depression found that taking up a new hobby helped them during their recovery, encouraging them to be more active and positive about life. Hobbies help us feel as though our lives are more eventful.

Want to encourage yourself to become more active in life? To have something to do? Does your life just consist of sleeping, eating and work or school? Well you know what they say...Get a hobby. Tips, tricks and tools for you to extend your skills and wellbeing.
